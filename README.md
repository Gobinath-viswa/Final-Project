## Domain

## E-Commerce / Retail Analytics

nalyze e-commerce sales data from 2024–2025

## Outcome

Clear understanding of sales and profit performance
Identification of top customers, products, and regions
Insights into optimal discount strategies
Clean and structured dataset ready for modeling
Business-ready insights for planning and forecasting

Descriptive Analysis (sales, profit summary)

## KEY INSIGHTS 

 Sales & Quantity 
 
 Sales increase steadily as order quantity increases, showing that bulk orders generate higher 
revenue. 
 Quantity level 5 contributes the highest sales value. 

# Orders Analysis 
 Order count is almost evenly distributed across all quantity levels (1–5). 
 This indicates consistent customer demand across different order sizes. 

# Regional Performance 
 North and South regions show higher contribution in unit price compared to other regions. 
 Regional performance varies, indicating the need for region-specific strategies. 

# Monthly Trend 
 Sales show seasonal fluctuations with noticeable peaks in certain months. 
 Mid-year months record higher sales growth, while some months show a decline. 

# Category Performance 
 Home Decor and Furniture are the top-performing categories. 
 Electronics, Clothing, and Books also contribute significantly. 
 Lower-performing categories like Toys and Beauty need improvement. 

# Payment Mode 
 Multiple payment options (COD, UPI, Cards, Net Banking) indicate diverse customer preferences. 
 Digital payments play a key role in order completion. 


## BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS 

# Promote Bulk Purchases 
Offer discounts or combo deals for higher quantities to increase revenue. 

# Focus on High-Performing Categories 
Increase inventory, marketing, and promotions for Home Decor and Furniture. 

# Improve Low-Performing Categories 
Introduce offers, bundles, or targeted ads for Toys and Beauty. 

# Region-Specific Strategy 
Invest more in North and South regions through localized campaigns. 

# Seasonal Planning 
Plan marketing campaigns around high-sales months and introduce offers during low-sales periods. 

# Encourage Digital Payments 
Provide cashback or rewards for UPI and card payments to improve transaction efficiency.


## Conclusion

This project shows that higher order quantities generate more sales, making bulk purchases a key revenue driver. Home Decor and Furniture are the top-performing categories, and the North and East regions contribute the most revenue. Overall, the dashboard proves how data analysis helps businesses make better, data-driven decisions.
